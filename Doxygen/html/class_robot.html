Logic to track whether the shooter is running and whether the shooter is at its maximum velocity

Logic to control the LED colors when shooting. Red and blue corresponding to the detected ball by the color sensor. Strobe = shooter ramping up. Solid = up to speed. Dual color = shooter not running.
